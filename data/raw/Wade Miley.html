Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Wade Miley?

Wade Miley is 38 years old.

When was Wade Miley born?

Wade Miley was born on November 13, 1986.

Where was Wade Miley born?

Wade Miley was born in Hammond, LA.

How tall is Wade Miley?

Wade Miley is 6-1 (185 cm) tall.

How much does Wade Miley weigh?

Wade Miley weighs 220 lbs (99 kg).

How many seasons has Wade Miley played?

Wade Miley is in his 15th season.

When was Wade Miley drafted?

Wade Miley was drafted by the Tampa Bay Devil Rays in the 20th round of the 2005 MLB June Amateur Draft from Loranger HS (Loranger, LA) and the Arizona Diamondbacks in the 1st round (43rd) of the 2008 MLB June Amateur Draft from Southeastern Louisiana University (Hammond, LA).

What position does Wade Miley play?

Wade Miley is a Pitcher.

How many strikeouts does Wade Miley have?

Wade Miley has 7 strikeouts this season and has 1,368 strikeouts over his career.

Wade Miley has played for 8 teams; the Arizona Diamondbacks, Baltimore Orioles, Boston Red Sox, Chicago Cubs, Cincinnati Reds, Houston Astros, Milwaukee Brewers and Seattle Mariners.

Wade Miley has never won a World Series.
