How old is Paul Goldschmidt?

Paul Goldschmidt is 37 years old.

Paul Goldschmidt was born on September 10, 1987.

Where was Paul Goldschmidt born?

How tall is Paul Goldschmidt?

Paul Goldschmidt is 6-2 (188 cm) tall.

How much does Paul Goldschmidt weigh?

Paul Goldschmidt weighs 225 lbs (102 kg).

How many seasons has Paul Goldschmidt played?

Paul Goldschmidt is in his 15th season.

When was Paul Goldschmidt drafted?

Paul Goldschmidt was drafted by the Los Angeles Dodgers in the 49th round of the 2006 MLB June Amateur Draft from The Woodlands HS (The Woodlands, TX) and the Arizona Diamondbacks in the 8th round of the 2009 MLB June Amateur Draft from Texas State University (San Marcos, TX).

What position does Paul Goldschmidt play?

Paul Goldschmidt is a First Baseman.

How many hits does Paul Goldschmidt have?

Paul Goldschmidt has 89 hits this season and has 2,145 hits over his career.

How many home runs does Paul Goldschmidt have?

Paul Goldschmidt has 8 home runs this season and has 370 home runs over his career.

Paul Goldschmidt has a .284 average this season and has a .289 average over his career.

Paul Goldschmidt makes $12,500,000.00 this year. His current contract terms are 1 yr/$12.5M (25).

Paul Goldschmidt has played for 3 teams; the Arizona Diamondbacks, New York Yankees and St. Louis Cardinals.

Paul Goldschmidt has never won a World Series.
