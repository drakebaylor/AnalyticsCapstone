How old is Lance McCullers?

Lance McCullers was born on March 8, 1964.

How tall is Lance McCullers?

Lance McCullers is 6-1 (185 cm) tall.

How much did Lance McCullers weigh when playing?

Lance McCullers weighed 185 lbs (83 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Lance McCullers play?

Lance McCullers played 7 seasons.

Is Lance McCullers in the Hall of Fame?

Lance McCullers has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Lance McCullers drafted?

Lance McCullers was drafted by the Philadelphia Phillies in the 2nd round of the 1982 MLB June Amateur Draft from Tampa Catholic HS (Tampa, FL).

How many strikeouts did Lance McCullers have?
