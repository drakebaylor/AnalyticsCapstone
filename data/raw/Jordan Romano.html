When was John Romano born?

John Romano was born on August 23, 1934.

John Romano was born in Hoboken, NJ.

How tall was John Romano?

John Romano was 5-11 (180 cm) tall.

How much did John Romano weigh when playing?

John Romano weighed 205 lbs (92 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did John Romano play?

John Romano played 10 seasons.

Is John Romano in the Hall of Fame?

John Romano has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

John Romano was a Catcher.

How many hits did John Romano have?
