Frequently Asked Questions

When was Mickey Harris born?

Mickey Harris was born on January 30, 1917.

Where was Mickey Harris born?

Mickey Harris was born in New York, NY.

How tall was Mickey Harris?

Mickey Harris was 6-0 (183 cm) tall.

How much did Mickey Harris weigh when playing?

Mickey Harris weighed 195 lbs (88 kg) when playing.

How many seasons did Mickey Harris play?

Mickey Harris played 9 seasons.

Is Mickey Harris in the Hall of Fame?

Mickey Harris has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

What position did Mickey Harris play?

How many strikeouts did Mickey Harris have?

Mickey Harris had 534 strikeouts over his career.
