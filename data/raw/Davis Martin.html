Dave Martinez played 16 seasons.

Dave Martinez has not been elected into the Hall of Fame.

When was Dave Martinez drafted?

Dave Martinez was drafted by the Texas Rangers in the 40th round of the 1982 MLB June Amateur Draft from Lake Howell HS (Winter Park, FL) and the Chicago Cubs in the 3rd round of the 1983 MLB January Draft-Secondary Phase from Valencia Community College (Orlando, FL).

What position did Dave Martinez play?

Dave Martinez was an Outfielder and First Baseman.

Dave Martinez had 1,599 hits over his career.

Dave Martinez had 91 home runs over his career.

Dave Martinez last played in 2001.
