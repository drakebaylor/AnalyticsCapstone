Frequently Asked Questions

How old is Chris Sale?

Chris Sale is 36 years old.

When was Chris Sale born?

Chris Sale was born on March 30, 1989.

Where was Chris Sale born?

Chris Sale was born in Lakeland, FL.

How tall is Chris Sale?

Chris Sale is 6-6 (198 cm) tall.

How much does Chris Sale weigh?

Chris Sale weighs 180 lbs (81 kg).

How many seasons has Chris Sale played?

Chris Sale is in his 15th season.

When was Chris Sale drafted?

Chris Sale was drafted by the Colorado Rockies in the 21st round of the 2007 MLB June Amateur Draft from Lakeland HS (Lakeland, FL) and the Chicago White Sox in the 1st round (13th) of the 2010 MLB June Amateur Draft from Florida Gulf Coast University (Fort Myers, FL).

What position does Chris Sale play?

Chris Sale is a Pitcher.

How many strikeouts does Chris Sale have?

Chris Sale has 114 strikeouts this season and has 2,528 strikeouts over his career.

How much is Chris Sale worth?

Chris Sale makes $22,000,000.00 this year. His current contract terms are 2 yrs/$38M (24-25) & 26 team option.

How many teams has Chris Sale played for?

Chris Sale has played for 3 teams; the Atlanta Braves, Boston Red Sox and Chicago White Sox.

How many World Series has Chris Sale won?

Chris Sale has won 1 World Series.

What are Chris Sale's nicknames?
