When was Aaron Judge drafted?

Aaron Judge was drafted by the Oakland Athletics in the 31st round of the 2010 MLB June Amateur Draft from Linden HS (Linden, CA) and the New York Yankees in the 1st round (32nd) of the 2013 MLB June Amateur Draft from California State University, Fresno (Fresno, CA).

What position does Aaron Judge play?

Aaron Judge is a Rightfielder.

How many hits does Aaron Judge have?

Aaron Judge has 119 hits this season and has 1,145 hits over his career.

How many home runs does Aaron Judge have?

Aaron Judge has 33 home runs this season and has 348 home runs over his career.

What is Aaron Judge's average?

Aaron Judge has a .360 average this season and has a .294 average over his career.

How much is Aaron Judge worth?

Aaron Judge makes $40,000,000.00 this year. His current contract terms are 9 yrs/$360M (23-31).

How many teams has Aaron Judge played for?

Aaron Judge has played for 1 team, the New York Yankees.

How many World Series has Aaron Judge won?

Aaron Judge has never won a World Series.

What is Aaron Judge's Instagram account?

Aaron Judge is on Instagram at thejudge44.

What are Aaron Judge's nicknames?

The Judge, All Rise, BAJ, Arson Judge are nicknames for Aaron Judge.
